LEGO Ideas

There are many lego Ideas. My personal favorite is the Saturn V Rocket and the Discovery Spaceship. LEGO Ideas was formerly known as CUUSOO, it is a Japanese partner of The LEGO Group. They work with the LEGO Group to produce community supported sets. If an idea submitted to the website gains 10,000 (originally 1,000) supporters, it stands a chance of being produced as an official LEGO product.
